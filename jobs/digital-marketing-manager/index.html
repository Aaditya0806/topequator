Descriptions

Overview

Gradient is a financial technology company that provides a revolutionary platform for businesses to access capital by transforming their recurring revenue streams into upfront capital. Founded in 2019, Gradient aims to redefine the traditional financing model by offering an innovative solution that allows companies to unlock the value of their predictable revenue.

Requirements

  • Digital Marketing Knowledge: Basic understanding of digital marketing principles, strategies, and best practices across various channels, including social media, email marketing, SEO, content marketing, and paid advertising.
  • Social Media Management: Familiarity with social media platforms, their features, and best practices for managing social media accounts, creating content, and engaging with the audience.
  • Content Creation: Basic knowledge of content creation and copywriting for digital channels, including writing engaging social media posts, blog articles, email campaigns, and website content.
  • Analytics and Reporting: Ability to analyze marketing campaign performance metrics using tools like Google Analytics, social media analytics, or other analytics platforms to assess the effectiveness of campaigns and make data-driven recommendations.
  • SEO Basics: Understanding of search engine optimization (SEO) concepts, including keyword research, on-page optimization, and link building strategies.
  • Email Marketing: Familiarity with email marketing platforms, list management, and basic knowledge of creating and optimizing email campaigns for lead generation and customer engagement.
  • Paid Advertising: Basic understanding of paid advertising platforms such as Google Ads, Facebook Ads, or LinkedIn Ads, and the ability to set up and manage basic ad campaigns.
  • Digital Marketing Tools: Proficiency in using digital marketing tools and platforms, such as social media management tools, email marketing software, content management systems (CMS), or marketing automation tools.
  • Communication Skills: Strong written and verbal communication skills to effectively convey marketing messages and collaborate with cross-functional teams, clients, and external stakeholders.
  • Creative Thinking: Ability to generate creative ideas for marketing campaigns, content, and strategies to engage the target audience and drive results.
  • Analytical Skills: Basic analytical skills to interpret data, identify trends, and provide insights for optimizing marketing strategies and campaigns.
  • Project Management: Basic project management skills to handle multiple marketing initiatives, meet deadlines, and prioritize tasks effectively.
  • Teamwork: Ability to work collaboratively with team members, agencies, or vendors to execute marketing campaigns and achieve marketing goals.
  • Adaptability: Willingness to learn and adapt to new digital marketing trends, technologies, and tools in a fast-paced and evolving digital landscape.
